Our Games

MG World Project

In a world where magic dictates power and social status, those without magical abilities are rejected and marginalized. Freyja’s village is destroyed, exposing her firsthand to the luxuries and power that mages truly possess. This drives her to seek change, both for herself and for her people.

A Fabulously Bad Girl

When a rival gang kidnaps her best friend, our fearless, rebellious heroine fights her way through the city’s most dangerous districts. Armed with bats, pipes, and whatever she can get her hands on, she’ll take on eccentric gangs, deranged bosses, and brutal street brawls. Can she reach the gang leader and save her friend before it’s too late?
